Hi there! It’s Grace and Levi here…but you probably already know that if you are reading this. What you might not know is the story of how we met. (Well, you might, but we’re still going to tell you anyway.)

We actually met online -- on Tinder of all places. Levi messaged Grace the line, “Your bio says Hufflepuff, but your picture says Gryffindor,” to which Grace launched into an explanation about how she was a Ravenclaw and asked him what his house was...little did she know he hadn’t even read the books (yet)! Regardless, if you know Grace you know that a Harry Potter pick up line was the best kind.

Then Levi invited Grace to a concert on June 10 at Kincaid Park. It was called “Alaska Summerfest” and it started around 3 p.m. and didn’t end until Night Ranger finished their set at around 10:30 p.m. (So, if Grace’s dad plays an obscene amount of Night Ranger songs at this wedding, you’ll know why!)

Anyway, we didn’t get tired of each other after that nearly 8-hour blind first date and from there, it’s history.

Almost two years later, Levi proposed to Grace while on vacation in England, in front of a bridge on the River Avon in Stratford-Upon-Avon. (That’s the picture on the top of the page...and for those of you asking, he used a tri-pod and put the camera on a timer under the rouse of taking a normal picture in front of the bridge.)

Please enjoy some of the pictures of our adventures above.

-Grace and Levi
