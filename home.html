COVID-19 UPDATE

Hello everyone!

We wanted to give everyone an update on how we are planning around the current coronavirus pandemic. We are closely monitoring the situation, and as of right now our wedding is planned to take place as scheduled.

If it seems as though it will not be safe to hold the wedding as planned by the end of May, we will postpone.

We realize many of you may have already RSVP-ed and are thinking about changing your travel plans. If that is the case please contact one of us! There will be absolutely no hard feelings if you can't make it to celebrate with us. Your physical, mental and financial health are much more important.

Also, if you're unable to attend, we plan on live streaming the ceremony for you to watch remotely if you'd like!

Stay safe everyone!
